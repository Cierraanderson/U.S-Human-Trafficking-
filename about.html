About This Platform

The Focus

This Platform focuses on addressing Human Trafficking Across America.

According to the Department of Justice, human trafficking is the illegal activity of forcing an individual to perform commercial sex acts or provide labor or other forms of service.

There can be covert or obvious, physical or psychological coercion. Whether or not coercion, fraud, or force were used, exploiting a minor for commercial sex is still considered human trafficking.

The Goal

The goal and mission for this platform is to help law enforcement and the community to know who they are looking for.

By putting visuals of missing victims, communities can look at this website and look for those they may know and help rescue the missing victims.

All visuals of the missing victims are in the 'Missing Person' nav when exiting this page.
